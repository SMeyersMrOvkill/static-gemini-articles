The Neverending Story Engine

Core Concepts

Metaphorical Bubbles as Dynamic Workspace

  • Inner Bubble: Immediate Narrative Possibilities
  • Middle Bubble: Relevant Memories & World Details
  • Outer Bubble: Generation of New Ideas & Conflicts

AI Roles

  • Exploration of Narrative Paths
  • Association & Connection Making
  • Inspiration & Idea Generation

Human-AI Collaboration

  • Human Author: Provides initial prompt, steers high-level direction
  • AI: Expands possibilities, generates options, ensures consistency

Key Components

Character/Entity Representation (JSON)

  • Properties: Active vs. Passive
  • Relationships: Dynamically Updated
  • Goals & Motivations: Driving Action
  • Beliefs & Values: Shaping Choices

World Building (External JSON/Database)

  • Lore & History: Accessed as Needed
  • Locations: Properties & Connections
  • Items & Objects: Significance & Properties

Implementation Details

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

  • Entity Recognition & Extraction
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • Relationship Extraction

Machine Learning (ML)

  • Predictive Text Generation
  • Plot Point Suggestion
  • Character Action Prediction
